Luciafesten hype!

A word from the party animals in charge

Welcome to Luciafesten, the cosiest party of the year. On the ninth of December Kårallen will be dressed to the nines with Christmas decorations on all floors, northen Europe's tallest Christmas tree and all the holiday spirit you could wish for. There will be Fun Little Stuff ™ placed all around Kårallen such as Tuba Hero, a Guitar Hero but with a tuba instead, and mysterious photo booths.

The evening starts off with a Christmas dinner in Gasquen before the party starts at eight o’clock. The evening will be filled with live music, no less than five bands will perform this lovely evening. We are proud to present Lusen Big Band, LiU-bandet, LiTHe Blås, Helmut Jederknüller mit seinem Super Stereo à Gogo Orchester and last but not least D-bandet! This will be a marvelous evening to say at the least. But the wonder does not end there, be a rockstar and join our fantastic live kakaoke band, Harakiri. When you are not singing your lungs out, you can take a break at one of our theme bars, take a swing around the Christmas tree or exchange a Christmas gift or two.

Sounds fun? To be able to do all the fun stuff we strongly recommend you to be there early to not miss anything. You know, early bird gets the worm! So leave your ovve at home, dress up in your loveliest Christmas outfit and join us at Kårallen on December ninth for a party you never will forget!

All love // the party animals in Christmas spirit.

Gustav, Tova and Sofia

Walking down memory lane

What the heck is LiTHe Blås?

I bet you have heard LiTHe Blås play sometime, and know that we are as fun watching as listening to. Perhaps you have seen the mysterious room next to Baljan? But do you know what happens in the orchestra during the year?

VSR

Party all day all night with live music and cheek-to-cheek dancing. In the middle we have a gig for all the competing students in Vallamassivet.

Valborg

Breakfast early in the morning, marching through city during day and partying all night. Sadly we have no time for watching the fire due to our busy schedule.

SOF

Every other year the Student Orchestra Festival, SOF, is in town, and LiTHe Blås is of course playing together with loads of other student orchestras. Three days of power party!

Eurotour

The tour is an experience unlike any other. A buss load of musicians lost in the rolling hills of Germany. Ten days of hardship, Weissbier, songs and a lot of bullshit, then we return as new people. Fun!

Nolle-p

Every year Campus is crowded with new, curious students and of course LiTHe Blås will be there to greet them with joy and entertainment.

Dra't i Spa't

Dra't i Spa't is a tug of war competition held every year. We lead the way for the competitors with our music down to Tinnerbäcken. Afterwards our own party begins and adventures awaits.

Finally it’s here! Live music, dance around the christmas tree, Lucia parade and of course, yours truly, LiTHe Blås! See you there!
